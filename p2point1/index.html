Project 2:Checkpoint 1

Shop 1 - The Near Future Co.

An online store that sells objects that are from the future that have time travelled to the present, literally. Items include high end fashion items, jewelry, and various tchotkes.

Shop 2 - The Meteorologist's Office

This shop sells bottled weather. If you want a sunny day, we got it. If you want a rainy day, we got it. If you want a snow day, we have that too!

Shop 3 - Flora with Feelings

This shop sells plants that speak back to you - every plant is unique and ready to be adopted in your home. Not sure if your plant needs water, sunlight, etc.? Just ask! We have sunflowers that love to tell jokes, roses that will be there for you when a first date goes wrong, and succulents that hold your secrets!
